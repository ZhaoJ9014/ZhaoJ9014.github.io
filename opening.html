Research Interests

Focusing on the major national demands and key scientific problems in the field, and aiming at the international frontiers and development trends, we are committed to build a research and development team on evolutionary vision+x oriented learning with international influence. Our goal is to break through bottlenecks and to make hardcore innovations. We dream big while being down-to-earth. We conduct scientific research and practical applications that are both groundbreaking and applicable, and are dedicated to provide new productive forces for “intelligence+” scenarios.

Our research interests mainly focus on AI Governance, i.e., Intrinsic Security, Derivative Security, Ethical Security, etc. We believe AI Governance is a global public good and urge collective action to address the AI frontier risks through transparent, collaborative risk management techniques and practices that can effectively mitigate risks while enabling beneficial AI development.
